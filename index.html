About JT Offroad

Clean installs. Honest recommendations. Trail-ready results.

JT Offroad helps drivers turn stock vehicles into capable adventure rigs. Whether you need a simple leveling kit or a full overland setup, every build is planned around how you actually use your vehicle.

Our goal is simple: build vehicles that are ready for the next trail, campsite, jobsite, and weekend escape.

What We Do

Services

Suspension & Lift Kits

Lift kits, leveling kits, shocks, coilovers, leaf packs, upper control arms, and full suspension systems.

Wheels & Tires

Wheel and tire packages, proper fitment, all-terrain tires, mud-terrain tires, and stance planning.

Armor & Protection

Bumpers, skid plates, rock sliders, bed protection, and underbody protection.

Lighting & Electrical

Light bars, ditch lights, pod lights, switch panels, auxiliary power, and clean wiring.

Recovery Equipment

Winches, recovery points, onboard air, recovery gear mounting, and trail-ready setup.

Overland Builds

Roof racks, bed racks, storage systems, camping accessories, and complete adventure rigs.

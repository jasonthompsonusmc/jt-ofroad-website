About

Northern California Off-Road Builds

JT Offroad builds practical, reliable, and trail-ready vehicles for people who want more than stock. From suspension upgrades to complete adventure builds, every setup is built around how the vehicle will actually be used.

Services

What We Build

Suspension & Lift Kits

Lift kits, leveling kits, shocks, coilovers, upper control arms, and complete suspension upgrades.

Wheels & Tires

Wheel packages, tire upgrades, fitment guidance, and off-road-ready setups.

Armor & Protection

Bumpers, skid plates, rock sliders, bed protection, and underbody armor.

Lighting & Electrical

Light bars, ditch lights, auxiliary lighting, switch panels, batteries, and clean wiring.

Recovery Equipment

Winches, recovery points, onboard air systems, and trail recovery setups.

Overland Builds

Roof racks, bed racks, storage, camping systems, and full adventure build planning.
